Location

You can access the park via the gate by the public toilet in Glebe Park, ACT.

Safari Man founded this park in 2002 following his controversial claims of dinosaur sightings in the Americas during the late 1980s. In hopes of starting fresh in the Southern Hemisphere, he bought a plot of land here in Canberra and transformed it into Dinosaur Park.
